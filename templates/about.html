Project Architecture

ECycle bridges the gap between consumers, organizations, and certified e-waste recyclers by leveraging Artificial Intelligence for device identification, carbon footprint reduction tracking, and reward incentives.

  • Real-time AI Inference: Powered by Computer Vision & Roboflow SDK models for device classification.
  • Automated Valuation: Dynamically calculates eco-point earnings & CO₂ reduction equivalent.
  • Disposal Certificates: Generates official PDF green disposal certificates for users & corporate bulk pickups.
  • Live Community: Interactive Twitter/X-style E-Talk feed with real-time polling updates.
