Important Information
Before Your Pickup:
  • Ensure your e-waste is easily accessible for our pickup team.
  • Remove any personal data from devices (factory reset recommended).
  • If possible, include original accessories and packaging.
  • For large items (TVs, refrigerators, etc.), please ensure there's clear access.
What Happens Next:
  1. Our team will arrive at your scheduled time.
  2. Your e-waste will be evaluated to confirm its condition.
  3. You'll receive a confirmation of pickup and eco points will be added to your account.
  4. Your e-waste will be properly recycled or refurbished according to environmental standards.
